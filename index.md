<section id="welcome" class="section">
  <h2>Welcome</h2>
  <p class="lead">
    On behalf of the organizing committee, we warmly welcome the community to
    IEEE AVSS 2027, the International Conference on Advanced Visual and
    Signal-Based Systems.
  </p>
  <p>
    AVSS provides an interdisciplinary forum for researchers, practitioners, and
    leaders from academia, industry, and government to exchange advances in
    theory, methods, systems, and applications. The conference covers visual
    analytics, signal-based perception, multimodal sensing, intelligent systems,
    privacy-aware deployment, and safety-critical real-world applications.
  </p>
  <p>
    AVSS 2027 will continue to highlight emerging directions in generative AI,
    foundation models, large language models, and trustworthy intelligent
    systems for visual and signal-based applications.
  </p>
</section>
